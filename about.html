Backpacking

One of the coolest experiences of my life was the backpacking trip that I took with my Dad, Brother, and a few friends to hike roughly 115 miles of the Continental Divide in Colorado between Pagosa Springs and Silverton. We decided during COVID that we were tired of being cooped up inside all day and planned this trip to the remote isolation of the Weminuche Wilderness.


After I graduated in May 2020, I wasn't sure that I was going to have anything to do over the summer, but this trip served as a "Senior Trip" of sorts and helped make COVID feel more normal. We trained and planned for several months before going and it was extremely strenuous, but it was an experience I will never forget and gave me memories to cherish for a lifetime.
